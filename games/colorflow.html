How to Play

Draw vibrant energy paths to securely connect all matching diamond crystals!

  • Draw: Touch a diamond and drag across the empty squares to connect it to its matching color companion.
  • Coverage: To earn ⭐⭐⭐ (3 stars), you must connect every single pair AND cover 100% of the entire grid with no empty squares left behind.
  • Overlap: Paths cannot cross. If you draw a new path over an existing one, the old path will be severed (overwritten).
  • Erase: Make a mistake? Simply tap the starting diamond of any completed path to instantly clear it.

⚡ Flow Bonus (Advanced):

Finding the shortest absolute distance between two diamonds while still filling the board will award you bonus points per pair!

How to Play ColorFlow 🎨

ColorFlow is a path connection puzzle. Connect matching colored dot pairs by drawing paths across the grid. Paths cannot cross or overlap!

🎯 Drawing Paths

  • Touch/click a colored dot and drag to draw a path.
  • Connect to the matching dot of the same color.
  • Drawing over another color's path overwrites it.
  • Tap a dot with a completed path to clear that color.

⭐ Star Ratings & Flow Bonus

  • ⭐: Solved but coverage < 80%
  • ⭐⭐: Solved with coverage ≥ 80%
  • ⭐⭐⭐: Solved with 100% coverage (all cells filled)
  • Flow Bonus: 100% coverage = 2× score!

💯 Scoring

200 base + coverage%×5 + speed bonus. No-hint bonus: ×1.3. Full coverage doubles your entire score!

💡 Hints

Use the 💡 Hint button to reveal 3-4 cells of the correct path for one color pair. First hint per session is FREE; subsequent hints require a reward ad.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I erase a line?

Trace backwards to shorten it, or tap either starting/ending dot of a completed path to clear that entire color's path instantly.

What is the Flow Bonus?

When you achieve 100% coverage (every cell filled), your entire score is doubled! This is the ultimate reward for perfect solutions.

How many levels are there?

5 packs with 30 levels each = 150 levels total. Grid sizes range from 5×5 to 9×9 with increasing numbers of color pairs.

Does using hints affect my score?

Yes! If you complete a level without using any hints, you earn a ×1.3 score multiplier. The first hint per session is free, subsequent hints require watching a reward ad.
