How to Play

Flip tiles to reach the target state. The game features 3 distinct modes:

  • Classic: Tap a tile to toggle it and its 4 adjacent neighbors (ON/OFF). Goal: Turn ALL tiles ON (glowing).
  • Cascade: Toggle effects propagate 2 levels deep (up to 13 cells at once). Think carefully!
  • Spectrum: Instead of ON/OFF, tiles cycle through 3 colors (Coral → Amber → Blue). Goal: Make all tiles the SAME color.

How to Play TileTurn 🔄

TileTurn is a strategic puzzle where every move causes a chain reaction. Navigate through three unique gameplay modes to test your logic.

🎮 Modes

  • Classic Mode: Turn all tiles ON. Tapping a tile flips its current state (ON to OFF, or OFF to ON) along with its direct top, bottom, left, and right neighbors.
  • Cascade Mode: Identical to Classic, but the flip effect penetrates twice as deep, creating complex patterns.
  • Spectrum Mode: Make the entire board a uniform color. Tapping cycles through three vibrant colors for the target and its neighbors.

⭐ Earning Stars

Every level has an optimally calculated "Minimum Moves" threshold. You are fully capable of making mistakes and trying again using the unlimited Undo button! To earn 3 Stars, complete the level in the exact minimum number of moves.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is every puzzle logically solvable?

Yes. Every single puzzle in the game is generated using the "Reverse Method." The game starts with a completed board and logically taps backwards to scramble it, guaranteeing that a solution always exists.

Does using the Undo button impact my star rating?

No! The Undo button actually subtracts a move from your total move counter, reverting you exactly to the state you were previously in. Feel free to use it constantly to test out different ripple patterns without penalty.

Do I have to unlock earlier packs to play later ones?

No, all 7 packs and their varied modes are unlocked from the start. However, within a specific pack, you must complete the levels in numerical order to unlock the next one.
