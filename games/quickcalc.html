How to Play QuickCalc ⚡

QuickCalc is a fast-paced mental math game that tests your calculation speed and accuracy. Answer equations correctly before the timer runs out!

🎯 Difficulty Phases

  • Phase 1 (Q1-5): Numbers 1-20, +/- only, 10s timer
  • Phase 2 (Q6-10): Numbers 1-50, +/-/×, 8s timer
  • Phase 3 (Q11-15): Numbers 1-100, all ops including ÷, 6s timer
  • Phase 4 (Q16+): Numbers 1-200, 2-step equations, 5s timer

🎰 Operator Roulette

From question 8+, sometimes the operator is hidden instead of the answer! Display: "7 ? 3 = 21" — you must identify "×". From question 15+, sometimes two operators are hidden for an even bigger challenge. Operator Roulette answers earn ×2 points!

💯 Scoring

  • Correct answer: 100 × current phase
  • Speed bonus: remaining seconds × 10
  • Streak bonus: consecutive correct × 50
  • Operator Roulette: ×2 points

💡 Hints

⏰ Timer Extension: Always FREE — adds +5s to your timer. 💡 Operator Hint: Narrows Operator Roulette to 2 choices. First use free, then requires reward ad.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the different game modes?

Classic: 3 lives, increasing difficulty. Wrong answers cost a life. Daily: Seeded sequence, one attempt per day, compete with everyone. Time Attack: 2 minutes, answer as many as possible, wrong = -3s penalty. Blitz: 30 seconds of rapid fire, maximum pressure!

How does the scoring work?

Each correct answer earns 100 × phase (1-4) points, plus a speed bonus (remaining seconds × 10) and a streak bonus (consecutive correct × 50). Operator Roulette answers earn ×2 points!

What is Operator Roulette?

From question 8+, sometimes the operator is hidden: "7 ? 3 = 21" — you must identify "×". From Q15+, two operators may be hidden. Use the 💡 hint to narrow down to 2 choices.

Are the wrong answers generated randomly?

No! There's one near miss (±1-3), one medium miss (±5-15), and one far miss. Careful!

How to Play

Test the speed and limits of your pure calculation skills!

  • Choose the correct answer (or operator) for the equation before the timer reaches 0.
  • Correct: Gain time (+2s) and points.
  • Wrong: Lose time (-3s) with a heavy screen shake.
  • Consecutive correct answers trigger "Combo Mode", significantly increasing your time reward up to +5s.
  • The difficulty automatically scales up as you progress. Survive as long as you can!
