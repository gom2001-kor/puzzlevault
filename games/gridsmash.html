How to Play GridSmash

GridSmash is a strategic block placement puzzle played on a 10×10 grid. Each turn you receive three block pieces of varying shapes and sizes. Drag and drop each piece onto the grid to place it — all three must be placed before you receive new ones.

Block Rotation: You can rotate any piece before or during placement! On desktop, right-click while dragging a piece to rotate it 90° clockwise. On mobile, simply tap a piece in the dock (without dragging) to rotate it. Tap multiple times to reach your desired orientation, then drag it onto the grid. Strategic rotation is key to fitting pieces into tight spaces!

When you completely fill a row or column, it clears from the board and earns you points. Clear multiple lines simultaneously for combo bonuses — the more lines at once, the higher the multiplier! Building consecutive combos across turns multiplies your score even further.

Starting from turn 10, special blocks may appear. Crystal blocks (💎) grant +200 points when part of a cleared line, also clearing the entire 3×3 area around them. Ice blocks (🧊) require 2 line clears to remove — the first clear cracks them, the second removes them. Wild blocks (⭐) are golden cells you can place anywhere, even on occupied cells.

Every 10 turns, the Shatter Zone activates on the bottom 2 rows for 3 turns. Line clears in the Zone score 3× points, but if the Zone expires without being cleared, 3 random cells fill with blocks. Master the Zone timing for massive scores! Clear the entire board for a Perfect Clear bonus of 5,000 points.

The game ends when none of the three new pieces can fit anywhere on the grid. Plan ahead, keep the board clean, and chase those combos. Try Zen Mode for stress-free play where pieces always fit.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the different block shapes in GridSmash?

GridSmash features 20 unique block shapes ranging from 1 to 5 cells. These include dots, lines, L-shapes, T-shapes, squares, plus signs, and more. Each turn you get 3 random pieces in different pastel colors.

How do special blocks work?

From turn 10 onward, special blocks may appear with each set of pieces. Crystal (💎) has a 15% chance and clears a 3×3 area when part of a cleared line (+200 pts). Ice (🧊) has a 10% chance and requires 2 line clears to remove. Wild (⭐) has a 5% chance and can be placed anywhere, even on occupied cells.

What is the Shatter Zone?

Every 10 turns, the bottom 2 rows become a golden Shatter Zone for 3 turns. Any line clears in these rows score 3× points. If the Zone expires without being cleared, 3 random cells fill with blocks as a penalty.

How is the score calculated?

You earn 10 points per cell placed, 100 per line cleared (×lines simultaneously), and combo streak multipliers (×1, ×1.5, ×2, ×3, ×5). Crystal block explosions give +200 points. Shatter Zone clears get 3× bonus. Perfect clears (empty board) earn +5,000 bonus!

What modes are available?

Classic: play until game over. Daily Challenge: same piece sequence for everyone with a 3-minute timer. Zen: no score or pressure, just relax. Sprint 40: race to clear 40 lines as fast as possible.
