How to Play

Create a fully functioning circuit board by connecting the power source to the battery.

  • Rotate: Tap any tile to rotate it 90° clockwise. Double-tap to quickly rotate 180°.
  • Energy Flow: The line will glow amber when it's actively connected to the source ⚡. Your goal is to chart the flow all the way to 🔋.

  • Locks: Tiles with a 🔒 cannot be rotated. You must build your circuit around them.

How to Play PipeLink 🔧

PipeLink is a circuit connection puzzle. Rotate pipe tiles to create a path from ⚡ Source to 💡 Target. Pipe types: straight(━), corner(┗), T-junction(┣), cross(╋), end cap(╸).

🔧 Circuit Board Theme

  • Tap any tile to rotate it 90° clockwise (smooth 200ms animation).
  • Connected pipes glow with energy flow animation (pulse from Source to Target).
  • Disconnected pipes are gray; connected ones glow blue.

⭐ Scoring

400 base + rotation efficiency bonus (solve ≤ optimal×1.5). No-hint bonus: ×1.2. Dual Source bonus: ×1.5.

💡 Hints

The 💡 Hint button rotates one pipe to its correct orientation (flashes gold first). First hint per session is FREE; subsequent hints require a reward ad.

Frequently Asked Questions

What do locked (🔒) tiles mean?

Locked tiles (Pack 5+) cannot be rotated. You must solve the circuit around these fixed constraints.

What is Dual Source mode?

From Pack 4+, two Source-Target pairs (A→A and B→B) must both connect simultaneously. Cross (╋) tiles can carry both energy paths independently.

How many levels are there?

5 packs with 25 levels each = 125 levels total. Grid sizes: 4×4, 5×5, 6×6, 5×5 (dual), 6×6 (dual + locked).

Does using hints affect scoring?

Yes! Completing without hints earns a ×1.2 score multiplier. First hint is free, subsequent ones require a reward ad.
