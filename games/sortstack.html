How to Play SortStack

SortStack is a relaxing yet challenging color sorting puzzle. Your goal is to sort all the scattered colored blocks so that each stack contains exactly 4 blocks of the same color.

Tap a stack to select its top block, then tap another stack to move the block there. You can only place a block onto an empty stack or onto a stack where the top block matches its color. A stack can hold a maximum of 4 blocks.

Lock & Expansion: SortStack features a unique mechanic where completing a stack (4 matching colors) locks it into place with a satisfying animation, and a new empty stack appears at the end of the line! This gives you progressively more workspace as you solve the puzzle.

Undo Freely: Made a mistake? Don't worry! You can use the Undo button as many times as you like without penalties or ads. Experimentation is part of the fun.

Hint System: Stuck? Use the 💡 Hint button to see the optimal next move. Your first hint each session is free; subsequent hints require watching a reward ad. Hints do not count as a penalty — but completing without hints earns a ×1.3 score bonus!

Scoring: Clear a level for 500 base points. On levels with move limits, earn bonus points for efficiency: (move_limit − moves_used) × 20. Complete without hints for a ×1.3 multiplier.

Move Limits: Easy mode has unlimited moves. Medium has 50, Hard has 40, and Expert has only 35. Plan your sorts carefully!

Frequently Asked Questions

How is SortStack different from other sorting games?

Instead of the typical test tubes and round balls, SortStack uses a clean rectangular drawer aesthetic with square blocks. Additionally, its unique "Lock & Expand" mechanic rewards you with a new empty stack every time you complete a color, creating a satisfying progression as you play.

Do I have to watch an ad to undo a move?

No! SortStack provides unlimited undos completely free. We believe puzzle games should be relaxing and forgiving of simple misclicks.

What are the different difficulty levels?

SortStack offers four difficulties in Classic mode: Easy (3 colors, 5 tubes, unlimited moves), Medium (5 colors, 7 tubes, 50 moves), Hard (7 colors, 9 tubes, 40 moves), and Expert (10 colors, 12 tubes, 35 moves). Relaxed mode removes move limits entirely. Daily Challenge uses a pre-selected difficulty that changes every day.

Are all puzzles solvable?

Yes. Every single puzzle in SortStack is generated by starting from a perfectly solved state and applying random reverse-moves. This guarantees that every layout is 100% solvable.

How to Play

Sort all colors so each stack has 4 blocks of the same color.

  • Tap a stack to lift its top block.
  • Tap another stack to drop the block.
  • You can only drop on the SAME color or an EMPTY stack.
  • Complete a stack (4 matching) to LOCK it and get an extra empty stack!
  • Hard/Expert levels have Move Limits.
