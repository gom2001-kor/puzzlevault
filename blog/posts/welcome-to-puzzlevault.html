Welcome to PuzzleVault — 10 Free Brain Games

February 15, 2026 · 3 min read

We're thrilled to officially welcome you to PuzzleVault — a collection of 10 unique, completely free puzzle games that run directly in your browser. No downloads required, no accounts to create, no subscriptions to worry about. Just pure puzzle-solving fun.

PuzzleVault was built on a simple idea: everyone deserves access to high-quality brain training games without barriers. Whether you're a casual player looking for a five-minute break or a serious puzzle enthusiast chasing perfect scores, there's something here for you.

Daily Challenges for Everyone

Every game features a Daily Challenge mode. Each day, a new puzzle is generated from a global seed — meaning every player worldwide gets the exact same puzzle. Complete your daily challenge, share your results, and see how your strategy compares to others.

Daily challenges reset at midnight UTC, and your streak is tracked automatically. Can you keep a 30-day streak alive?

Built for the Modern Web

PuzzleVault is a fully static Progressive Web App (PWA). All games are built with vanilla HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript — no heavy frameworks, no app store installations. Games load instantly, work offline, and your progress is saved locally on your device. Add PuzzleVault to your home screen and it works just like a native app.

We believe puzzle games should be accessible, free, and beautiful. That's the promise of PuzzleVault. Pick a game, challenge yourself, and enjoy the vault.
