About PuzzleVault

PuzzleVault is a collection of 10 free, browser-based puzzle games designed to challenge your mind every day. No downloads, no accounts, no ads during your first few games — just pure puzzle fun.

Our Mission

We believe great puzzle games should be accessible to everyone. Every game on PuzzleVault runs 100% in your browser with no server required. Your progress is saved locally on your device.

Features

  • 10 unique puzzle games with original mechanics
  • Daily challenges with global leaderboards
  • Works offline as a Progressive Web App
  • No account needed — play instantly
  • Share your results with friends
