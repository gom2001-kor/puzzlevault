About PuzzleVault

Welcome to PuzzleVault, your ultimate destination for free, high-quality, and ad-supported puzzle games on the web.

Our mission is simple: to provide engaging and brain-teasing puzzle experiences that are instantly accessible to everyone. We believe that gaming should be frictionless, which is why all games run 100% in your browser — no downloads, no accounts, and no data collection required.

Built for the Web

PuzzleVault is built entirely with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We rely on standard Web APIs like Canvas and Web Audio to deliver smooth, 60fps gameplay and rich sound effects without the heavy overhead of modern JavaScript frameworks or external libraries. This ensures that our games load instantly and run perfectly on both desktop and mobile devices.

Our Games

We've meticulously designed each of our 10 puzzle games to feature original visual aesthetics and unique gameplay mechanics that you won't find anywhere else. From solving logical codes to mastering grid manipulation, there is always a new challenge waiting for you in the Vault.

Every game features Daily Challenges — a new seed-based puzzle each day that is the same for everyone worldwide. Track your streaks, sharpen your skills, and compete with yourself to beat your personal bests.

Privacy & Terms

We respect your privacy. All your game progress, high scores, and settings are saved locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. For more details on how we handle data and manage our ad-supported model, please review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
